When to use ODIYA

🚗
Car accident abroad
You're in a car accident in the US but can't explain your location in English. Open ODIYA, tap the button, and read the English script to 911.
🏔
Lost while traveling
You're hiking or traveling in an unfamiliar area and need to tell someone exactly where you are. ODIYA gives you a precise address and GPS coordinates in the local language.
🚨
Medical emergency
Someone is hurt and you need an ambulance, but you don't know the local address or emergency number. ODIYA detects your country and shows the right number to call.
📱
Share location with family
Tap "Share" to instantly send your exact location to family or friends via text message, including a Google Maps link.
